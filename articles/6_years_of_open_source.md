# 6 years of Open Source 

6 years have flown by! Making Shuffle open source all those years ago was a great way to learn what the security market really needs. And today I am here to, yet again, brag about what the Shuffle team and community has achieved over the past few years, which I am immensely proud of!

Here are some highlights from building B2B software in cybersecurity:

* Shuffle has 2M downloads of the open source version of Shuffle
* We have had about 20k signups over the last 4 years on our cloud (~100/week average)
* These visitors are from 150+ countries
* Our [partners and customers](https://shuffler.io/partners) are all over the globe
* Our team has grown to 17 people, all over the world

I still don’t fully grasp the growth we have had. And the reach is still growing! Our team still fully owns Shuffle as well, allowing us to [experiment](https://singul.io), without the pure rush to grow as fast as possible without reason. 

And we have a new open source experiment happening: [Shuffle Security](https://github.com/shuffle/shuffle-security). It is a new UI, built on Shuffle’s backend automation technology, living next to the workflow-builder of Shuffle Core. It is built to solve the same problem, but from the angle of Security Operations rather than automation. It is a pure-play security platform, with workflows doing the automation, ensuring full transparency and that you stay in control, whether you use it normally or want to use [AI Agents](https://security.shuffler.io/agents) to handle your incidents for you.

<img width="754" height="158" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/126cddda-f2a8-4394-a17d-7558caad3e32" />

Now to the fun part: We want you to modify it. The goal with Shuffle Security is two-fold:
1. Give you an automated, multi-tenant security alert management system
2. Give you the option to modify it however you like. That means branding, naming... everything is up to you.

Shuffle Security has many “hidden” parts we will announce over time as well, such as Vulnerability Automation, Shadow Agent tracking & detection management across your tools. As it’s all built on top of Shuffle Core, we can expand these capabilities quickly, with the Shuffle Security supporting the same 3000+ integrations. 

Thanks for being a part of the journey! 

Fredrik, Founder & Lead Developer
